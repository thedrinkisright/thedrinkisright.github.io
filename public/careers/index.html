Now Hiring: Bartender (Part-Time/On-Call)

Apply Here

We're looking for experienced, personable bartenders to join our dynamic mobile bartending team servicing private events across all five boroughs of New York City and New Jersey.

This is a part-time/on-call position — perfect for someone looking to earn additional income on the side. Most of our events take place on weekends, with occasional weekday gigs. Many of our team members have full-time day jobs, so this is a great fit if you're seeking flexible, supplemental work.

Requirements

  • Valid driver's license and reliable vehicle (must be willing to travel to events)
  • Bartender's license from an accredited bartending school or at least 1 year of high-volume bartending experience
  • Strong knowledge of standard cocktail recipes and pour accuracy
  • Excellent customer service skills
  • Friendly and professional demeanor
  • Ability to multitask, stay calm under pressure, and work in fast-paced environments
  • Willingness to travel — the further you're able to go, the more bookings you'll receive

Duties Include

  • Set up and break down of the mobile bar at event locations
  • Mixing and serving drinks efficiently and accurately
  • Providing excellent customer service and engaging with guests
  • Handling cash tips and keeping bar area clean and organized
  • Maintaining inventory control during events
  • Adhering to safety and alcohol service regulations

Additional Info

  • Pay is Hourly + Tips
  • Pay may vary depending on event type and responsibilities
  • After hire, bartenders are required to:
    • Complete an online TIPS certification course
    • Purchase standard bartending tools (pourers, shaker, tip jar, and ice bucket) — all other tools provided by the company

If you're a skilled bartender with a flexible schedule, a positive attitude, and a car — we'd love to hear from you!
