What We Offer

Bar Services

You provide the spirits — we handle everything else.

🍾

How it works: The Drink Is Right operates as a Dry For-Hire bartending service. All alcoholic beverages are purchased by you (the client) from any licensed liquor store. We arrive with a professional bartender, all equipment, mixers, garnishes, and everything needed to make your event unforgettable.
